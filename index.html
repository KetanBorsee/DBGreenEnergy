About Us

Founded in 2020, Dodheshwar Biofuels aims to transform the energy industry by providing high-quality biomass briquettes. Its vision is a world that does not rely much on fossil fuels but on agricultural waste while the environment is protected and sustained through eco-friendly biofuels. Since its inception, as per the vision of the company's founder, Shri S.K. Dayma, who has over 55 years of experience in construction, the company has always been focused on providing the best quality and performance.

The people spearheading Dodheshwar Biofuels have vast experience in other industries like construction and education. Switching to the briquette manufacturing sector focused on environmental preservation wasn't easy, but it was significant. To identify the sources of pollution, we conducted a survey. Although there were multiple contributing factors, industries' use of fossil fuels emerged as the primary cause. This understanding fueled our dedication to making a difference.

Corn Cob Briquettes

Benefits of choosing this product:


High Energy Efficiency :

At 3600 KJ/KG GCV, our briquette biomass products guarantee optimum performance, thus translating into minimal fuel usage.

Eco-Friendly :

Through the use of agricultural waste, pollution and fossil fuel usage decrease.

Reliable Quality :

Ash content and moisture content, for instance, are measures of consistency across various uses of the coal.

Production Process :

These agricultural waste briquettes are made by a careful process that involves gathering agricultural corn waste, drying it, and then carving it into a briquette. This process guarantees the densification and enhancement of their energy production, making them reliable sources of fuel. The briquettes are intended to burn well, without polluting the environment with soot and other black products that cause the emission of CO2 into the atmosphere.

Handling and Storage Tips :

For these briquettes to have a low moisture content and be fit for use, they should be stored in a dry area. They should not be stored in wet places that may cause them to absorb moisture that alters their ability to burn and reduces their energy output.

Advantages over Coal :

Corn cob briquettes have several environmental benefits over the use of coal. They are renewable forms of energy, hence making them friendly to the environment since they are a form of "green energy." Their utilization helps in reducing the usage of fossil fuels, hence making them a better option for use both in industries and at home.

FAQ'S

What raw materials are used in your biomass briquettes? +
The biomass briquettes produced at Dodheshwar Biofuels, a top-notch briquettes manufacturing company, are made from agricultural residues, which include corn cobs and cotton stalks. These materials are then refined to come up with a renewable and effective energy source.
What is the Gross Calorific Value (GCV) of your briquettes? +
The gross calorific value of our briquettes varies by type. As for the â€˜energy value per kilogram' of corn cob briquettes, it is 3600 KJ/KG and cotton stalk briquettes are 3200 KJ/KG.
What is the moisture content of your briquette biomass? +
A moisture content of 10% is observed in corn cob and cotton stalk briquettes, which is thus good for combustion and performance.
What is the ash content of your briquettes? +
The ash content of our briquettes is carefully controlled: corn cob briquettes contain about 10 percent ash, while the ash content of cotton stalk briquettes is estimated to be about 12 percent.
Can your briquettes be provided in bag packing? +
Indeed, our biomass briquettes are packaged in bags. Like the previously described options, this packaging option is also convenient for transport and storage in a range of properties.

As for the Storage Factor (SF) ratio of our briquette biomass, it is not constant as it is adjusted for particular ultimate uses and storage conditions.
